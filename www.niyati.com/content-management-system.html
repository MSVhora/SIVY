Content Management System


Web and Enterprise Content Management Solution for today’s businesses.
Home / Application Development / Content Management System
Store, manage and share any media type, from Word docs to PDFs and rich media files like audio and video.

The enterprise world is under a deluge of unstructured data floating around. Strewn across folders, computers and office locations are content that are critical for day-to-day business functioning. How would you collect, organize, archive and retrieve these data in a fraction of a second? Here’s where a well-executed Content Management System can help you.


Get started

Our CMS Services Include the Following:


Web Content Management (WCM)

Easily create, manage and publish website content enabling rapid site deployment. Deliver a captivating online experience in the language of your choice and get the content displayed optimally on desktop, tablet and mobile devices. Embed rich media content, complex database systems and enhanced search functionalities.

Enterprise Content Management (ECM)

Go beyond just storing and retrieving digital content. Get a seamless enterprise platform to manage, process, access, integrate and measure all your critical systems and content. Manage information flow across organization with well-defined access policies. Work smarter, faster with content organized and deployed on your premise or on the cloud.

Digital Asset Management (DAM)

From simple documents to rich media files like photos, videos, audio and animation, you need a scalable and secure enterprise platform to manage all your digital content. With our DAM solutions, enterprises and digital agencies can create and manage multichannel marketing campaigns. The software in deployed on the cloud, saving substantial cost, resources and time.

Records Management (RM)

Manage the records of your organization efficiently through it entire lifecycle – from creation of the record till its disposition. This helps in streamlining all the records to be stored, distributed, maintained and disposed in a timely and effective manner. Ensure compliance and control of all records across departments.
