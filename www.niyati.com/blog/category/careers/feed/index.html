Niyati is looking for Mobile App Developers, specifically in the iOS platform, at its Chennai Office. This is a full-time job position with attractive salary for the candidate with the right experience. You must be an expert in tools and languages (Objective C, XCode, Java, and related IDEs) to design and develop both native as well as hybrid mobile applications and games for iPhone, iPad and iPad mini devices. Your knowledge should span the entire spectrum and workflow of mobile application development starting from UI Design Architecture to coding and development as well as App Store Submission and optimization.

We are looking for candidates with at least one live implementation at the App Store. Needless to say, you should be a team player and willing to work with a small team and help build on our strengths and capabilities.

Freshers with the relevant software skills may also apply at Trainee Level. You may email your resume directly to info [at] niyati dotcom or call 98840 42639 for any queries.
