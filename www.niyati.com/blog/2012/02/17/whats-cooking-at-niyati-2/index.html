What’s Cooking at Niyati

By  Ramesh Nair   |   Feb 17, 2012   |   Post Comments
   

These are exciting days for us at Niyati. The New Year has brought us an array of projects that will challenge both of our creative and coding expertise. Here are few projects we are currently working on:

A daily deals portal

Did we hear a collective sigh? No we are not replicating or cloning Groupon  – in design or functionality! A coding intensive project, we are building what we believe will be a game-changer in the flooded daily deals market.

A travel portal

This long-term project is to build the largest travel portal for a fast developing African country.

An educational portal

This project from a Singapore client is very close to our heart and has really taken off in scope ever since we started working on it.

A music portal

This foot tapping, wonderful project came through a client referral. The portal is aiming to take traditional Indian music, global

Naming and branding

Our maverick name developers have their hands full with names to be developed for a global electronics brand, a mining company, an online portal and a food brand. Our logo designers are working overtime to get the branding right for a corporate company, a software product, an online portal and more.

Digital marketing

We are helping a popular relationship counsellor based in NY sell her recently launched book. Our digital marketing skills are also helping clients in a myriad of industry verticals – jewellery, fitness equipment, musical instruments and medical equipment and building brand equity through social media and other online channels.

Website development

Our website designers and developers are busy giving finishing touches for websites that include a bio-tech company, a builder, a multi-faceted business group, a medical technology company, a life sciences company to name a few.

However, it is not all work and no time for creativity for the team! In our free time, we are conceptualizing a few software product ideas which we will be ready to launch by the mid-year.

 

As you can see, we have our plates full and need the best hands in the market to deliver World class quality to our wonderful customers. Should you be interested to join the team at Niyati,  this is the right time!
