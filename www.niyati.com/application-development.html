Application Development



The inter-connected enterprise is always on-the-go.

Evolving business challenges require technology solutions that are adaptive and scalable as your business grows. At Niyati, we specialize in delivering custom application development solutions that can automate your business processes and provide a superior ROI to your technology investments.


Get started

Our service offerings include:

Application Management & Migration

 Our ongoing application maintenance and management services focus on maximizing your existing applications and minimizing downtime. We can help modernize your legacy systems to keep pace with growing business needs and improve efficiencies.

SMAC Solutions

 Social, Mobile, Analytics and Cloud (SMAC) has changed the digital landscape like no other. By embracing SMAC framework, businesses can now create an ecosystem that accelerates their digital transformation. This results in enhanced operational efficiency and improved customer engagement.

CMS & Portal Development

 We develop web and enterprise content management (ECM) solutions for businesses to capture, store, distribute and archive content across platforms and mediums. Our portal development solutions include ecommerce portals, informational portals and intranet/extranet portals.

Digital Asset Management (DAM)

 We help you organize, preserve and archive all your digital assets in a structured way so that they can be easily located and retrieved using powerful search functionality.
