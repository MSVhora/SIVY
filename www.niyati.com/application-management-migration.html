Our Application Management solutions include:


Varied applications. Unified needs.

The changing dynamics of the marketplace results in enterprises dealing with dozens of applications. Our application management services are focused towards providing cost effective solutions that can be quickly deployed and offers tremendous value to your technology investments.

Modernization of legacy applications.

Moving your slow-performing and resource-intensive systems to emerging technologies and platforms is inevitable. We can help you make the move seamlessly.

Migration to the cloud.

With cloud computing gaining traction due to its affordability and versatality, enterprises big and small are moving towards a server-less office environment. However successful transition of your apps or data to cloud can be tricky, unless handled well. Niyati can help you to be fully virtual organization by identifying a preferred cloud environment and moving your on-premise enterprise server to the cloud.

Integration and Migration to Mobile.

With businesses taking the mobile first strategy, several web applications require either a migration or seamless integration with the social or mobile platforms. Niyati brings the technology knowhow and expertise to meet these migration requirements for your web application. We have dedicated teams proficient in developing apps on both iOS and Android platforms.
