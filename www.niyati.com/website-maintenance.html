Website Maintenance


Keep your website fresh and updated with regular maintenance!

Providing support service to keep your website prim and proper!

A website that is rarely updated is much more than an eyesore - it reflects on the professionalism of your organization. The primary reason for several websites lying unattended is the due to the lack of web professionals inside the organization. This is where Niyati can help.

Why Niyati for your website maintenance?

A website maintenance agreement with us will result in you having an immaculate website that serves its purpose remarkably well. Apart from updating your content on a regular basis, we can also cleanup the design, check coding errors and work towards improving the visibility and visitor count of the website through our specialized search engine optimization services.

If your website depends on online community participation, we can also manage your social media profiles including Facebook and Twitter.
Our website package includes the following:
  • Website content updation
  • Adding new sections, content, images, animations etc
  • Debugging of errors in pages, links, forms etc.
  • Converting desktop-only sites to responsive framework
  • Search Engine Optimization, link building activities and delivering ranking reports
  • Email newsletter creation and maintenance
  • Shopping cart product updates, product data entry
  • Social media page management
  • Image, icon, graphic design works
  • FTP management

Our USP’s in website maintenance:
  • We can have a dedicated person (or team, for larger clients) to take care of all your updates, saving you from hiring professionals in-house
  • Our response time to you support request is considerably fast
  • We employ only well-trained professionals, so that the turnaround time is much faster
  • We provide entire spectrum of website maintenance services – from graphic design, to HTML coding, database programming, social media management and digital marketing. This will save you from co-ordinating with multiple vendors each month
  • We take data security seriously. All projects are covered by a NDA and your details are accessed only by select personnel
  • As our development center is located in India, our services are cost-effective. Apart from the annual maintenance contracts we also offer one-time maintenance support for any bug testing or website auditing
  • We open a seamless communication channel with your team. We can be reached over phone, email or Skype
  • There is no long term contract. You can cancel the website maintenance agreement at any time with a month's notice.

Website maintenance cost:


As each customer’s requirement differs, we don’t have website maintenance packages offering fixed monthly pricing. The costing usually depends on your job description, number of personnel we allocate and their expertise.
